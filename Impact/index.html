About Us

SMK Telkom Sandhy Putra Purwokerto

Two majors were developed at Telkom Purwokerto Vocational School, namely the PPLG and TJKT majors. PPLG is a major that studies software development methods, including creation, maintenance, management, organization and development. Meanwhile, the TJKT department contains 2 other different departments, namely TJA and TKJ. TKJ is a major that studies how to assemble computers and install computer networks. TJA is a department that is competent in the field of networks, both cable (copper, coaxial and fiber optic) and wireless access networks.



Located on Jl. D.I Panjaitan number 128, Karangreja, Purwokerto Kulon, Kec. South Purwokerto, Kab. Banyumas, Central Java, there is the Telkom Sandhy Putra Purwokerto Vocational High School. This school is also located in the Telkom Education Foundation area, in the same place as the Telkom Purwokerto Junior High School and the Telkom Purwokerto Education Institute. Built on January 30 1993, this school has won various championships to be proud of. This school is a gathering place for outstanding teachers and students to develop themselves and support each other.
